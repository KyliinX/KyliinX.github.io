Co-op: AI-Based Wildfire Detection System Design

Hubei State Grid Wuhan University of Technology Power

 Posted on May 1, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  677 words  |  

During my internship from May 2024 to August 2024 at Hubei State Grid Wuhan University of Technology Power Consulting Division, I had the opportunity to engage with cutting-edge technologies in digital grid solutions, intelligent energy, and extensive data operations. The company specializes in integrating digital technology into power grid solutions, providing intelligent energy, system integration, and comprehensive data analytics expertise. This internship allowed me to work on several impactful projects, significantly enhancing my technical and professional skills.

Key Achievements

  • AI-Based Wildfire Detection System:

    • One of the significant projects I worked on was the AI-based transmission line wildfire detection system. I was responsible for data processing and algorithm modeling. Using surveillance cameras and LabelImg, we collected and labeled video frames, incorporating meteorological data for enhanced analysis. I preprocessed the data by applying grayscale and normalization techniques, improving the input data’s quality and consistency.
    • I used MobileNetV2 as a pre-trained model for the model construction, enhancing it with the SE Block attention mechanism to focus the model on fire regions. Additionally, I integrated a time-series LSTM module to help the model capture the dynamic features of fire development over time. Through multi-task learning, the model could detect both smoke and flames, achieving an 87.3% accuracy rate in wildfire detection, significantly improving its practical applicability.

  • Tool Development for Efficiency:

    • Besides developing algorithms, I created several utility programs to improve team efficiency. For example, I used C++ to develop batch file renaming tools, Excel file batch processing utilities, and automated data validation and correction tools. These tools drastically reduced the time spent on routine data tasks.validation and correction tools. These tools drastically reduced the time spent on routine data tasks.
    • I also utilized Git to manage project files and code versions, ensuring smooth collaboration and keeping track of changes to ensure project consistency.

  • Collaboration and Reporting:

    • I also participated in client communication, helping to manage client expectations, gather feedback, and document requirements into reports for the engineering team. This experience gave me insights into handling ambiguous information and effectively communicating technical concepts with non-technical stakeholders.

  • Power Distribution System Design and Documentation:

    • I was responsible for creating and standardizing the power distribution system’s topology diagram using Visio, ensuring the diagrams were transparent, standardized, and visually appealing. I also contributed to editing and improving product manuals, making sure the documentation adhered to the company’s standards and accurately conveyed technical details.

What I Learned

  • Deep Learning and Computer Vision:

    • This internship deepened my understanding of computer vision and deep learning. I gained hands-on experience with advanced neural networks and algorithms, particularly in real-time video data processing and wildfire detection, a unique application of AI technology.

  • Programming and Tool Development:

    • I sharpened my programming skills in C++ and Python, and became proficient in tools like Git and Excel for data handling. I also improved my ability to write and implement efficient code to automate tasks, which was crucial in improving team productivity.

  • Client Interaction and Communication:

    • This internship was an excellent opportunity to develop my communication skills. I learned to understand client needs, handle feedback, and translate these insights into actionable reports. Effective communication became a cornerstone of my work, which will be invaluable as I progress in academic and professional settings.

  • Visualization and Reporting:

    • I also learned to work with Visio for drawing standardized diagrams and gained experience in editing and polishing documentation, which helped refine my attention to detail and ability to clearly communicate technical information.

Conclusion

My time at Hubei State Grid Wuhan University of Technology was a pivotal experience, enhancing my technical and soft skills. The internship not only strengthened my understanding of AI-based solutions in the energy sector but also allowed me to develop tools and systems that significantly improve operational efficiency.

I plan to continue expanding my knowledge in data analysis and artificial intelligence. My ultimate goal is to apply these skills in deep learning and contribute to cutting-edge research, particularly in wildfire detection systems. As I look forward to my next internship, I aim to further enhance my technical expertise and prepare for a future in AI-driven research.
